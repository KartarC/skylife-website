How We Work

Our Process

01
Initial Consultation
We start with a conversation — your goals, timeline, budget, and mission profile. Whether buying or selling, we tailor our approach to your specific situation before making any recommendations.
02
Market Research & Strategy
We analyze the current Canadian and North American market, identify opportunities or comparable listings, and build a strategy — whether that's a listing plan for sellers or a search plan for buyers.
03
Active Execution
For sellers: professional listing, marketing to our buyer network, viewings management. For buyers: sourcing aircraft on and off market, arranging viewings, coordinating inspections.
04
Negotiation & Due Diligence
We negotiate on your behalf and manage the due diligence process — pre-purchase inspection, maintenance records review, lien searches, and financing confirmation.
05
Close & Transfer
Escrow, bill of sale, Transport Canada registration transfer, and final funds release. The deal closes cleanly with both parties protected.
