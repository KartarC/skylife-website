Our Story

Built From Passion

SkyLife Aircrafts was founded with a mission to bring real transparency to the Canadian private aviation market. Too many buyers were overpaying for aircraft with undisclosed issues. Too many sellers were leaving money on the table because they didn't know what their aircraft was worth.

We built SkyLife to fix that. Starting from zero in Mississauga, Ontario, we set out to create a brokerage that treats every client — whether their budget is $80,000 or $8 million — with the same professionalism and care.

Our long-term vision is to grow beyond brokerage — into charter, fleet management, and eventually into the airline space. But right now, we're focused on being the most trusted name in Canadian private aircraft transactions.
